Characteristics of an Scorpio

Scorpio-born are passionate and assertive people. They are determined and decisive, and will research until they find out the truth. Scorpio is a great leader, always aware of the situation and also features prominently in resourcefulness. Scorpio lives to experience and express emotions. Although emotions are very important for Scorpio, they manifest them differently than other water signs. In any case, you can be sure that the Scorpio will keep your secrets, whatever they may be.



Love Match

Rules by fiery Mars and destructive Pluto, Scorpio has one mode for relationships and that's full-on. Scorpio loves romance and passion, including up-all-night fights. Scorpio never does anything halfway, and believes that true love comes with true fights.

Career & Money

Intensity. Scorpio is much happier leading the pack or providing an authentic opinion than being a team player. When Scorpio goes in, they go all in.

Health

Intense workout classes. Scorpio can be almost masochistic when it comes to exercise, and loves working out harder, longer, and faster than anyone else.
